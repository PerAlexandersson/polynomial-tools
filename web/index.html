SymCat Real-rootedness ☕ Support

Polynomial Tools

Real-rootedness, interlacing, and recurrence search — runs locally in your browser via WebAssembly.

Examples:
iAutomatically tries increasingly complex recurrence shapes (depth, degrees, derivatives) starting from the simplest. Stops at the first match. Usually the best choice. iSet exact search bounds yourself. Useful when you know the expected recurrence shape or want to explore a specific search space.
iMaximum number of previous terms P(n−1), P(n−2), … the recurrence may reference. iMaximum degree in the polynomial variable t for each coefficient a_k(n, t). iMaximum degree in the index n for each coefficient a_k(n, t). iMaximum order of the derivative operator D = d/dt applied to terms. Allows recurrences involving P(n−k), P′(n−k), P″(n−k), etc.
iAllow an additive term g(n, t) on the right-hand side: ∑ a_k(n,t) P(n−k) = g(n, t). Slower search but finds more recurrences. iAllow a common denominator f(n, t) on the left-hand side: f(n, t) P(n) = ∑ a_k(n, t) P(n−k). Finds recurrences with rational function coefficients in n and t.
